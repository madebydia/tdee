Where the numbers come from

This mirrors the linked calculator's Harris-Benedict-style imperial BMR equations: male BMR = 66 + 6.23w + 12.7h - 6.8a; female BMR = 655 + 4.35w + 4.7h - 4.7a. TDEE multiplies BMR by the selected activity factor. Results are estimates, not medical advice.
